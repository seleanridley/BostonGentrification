More about this project!

Grass is greener on the gentrified side

Our tactical media project topic is gentrification. We created a parody website about gentrification in a couple of specific areas in Boston like Chinatown, South End, and Roxbury.
The purpose of this website is to bring more awareness on the topic of gentrification by creating fake advertisement of places that we believe could be potentially gentrified, places that have already been gentrified, and places that are currently being gentrified in the Boston area.
The design of our website will incorporate elements of house hunting websites which will be used to promote areas that have been gentrified. Also, before and after photographs of gentrified areas with a description of the old and new buildings will be included in the website.
We will use resources from websites like ​Dealing with Gentrification​ and ​Governing.com ​for information related to gentrification.
